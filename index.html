Lewis Hamilton

The greatest driver of all time

Here is a list of some of Lewis Hamilton's majors achievements:

  • 2003 - He won the Formula Renaul 2.0 UK Championshhip.

  • 2004 - Finished 1st in front of Nico Rosberg in the only Bahrain Superprix ever held.

  • 2005 - He won the Formula 3 Euro Series Championship and the Masters of Formula 3 Race.

  • 2006 - Won the GP2 Series Championship.

  • 2007 - Made his F1 debut with Mclaren and became the first and only black driver in F1.

  • 2008 - Won his first F1 title with Mclaren finishing the season with 10 podiums , including 5 wins.

  • 2014 - Switched over as a Mercedes driver for the new hybrid engine era.

  • 2014-2015 - He won the championship 2 years inn a row with a total of 21 wins over both years.

  • 2016 - He finished second , to his teammate, finishing second by only 5 points.

  • 2017-2020 - He then proceeded to win 4 years in a row, for a total of 7 titles, matching the world record of most title ever won in F1.

  • 2025 - Secured a seat at Ferrari for the upcoming year.
